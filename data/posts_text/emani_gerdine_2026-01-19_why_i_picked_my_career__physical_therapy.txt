Why I Picked My Career: Physical Therapy
Finding a career path is often a personal journey. For me, it started with my family. My mother had to undergo several knee surgeries, and I was by her side for almost every step of her recovery.
Being present for the majority of her physical therapy appointments gave me a front-row seat to the healing process. I watched her go from being unable to walk to regaining her full strength. This experience sparked a deep interest in me; I realized I wanted to be the person who helps others regain their mobility and independence.
In the context of my blog, The Agency Paradox, physical therapy is the perfect example of reclaiming one's agency through the help of a professional guide.
