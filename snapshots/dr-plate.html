Overview

Dr. Plate's instructor blog models argumentative engagement with AI critics while providing intellectual foundations for student work. Core thesis: AI coding adaptations—plan mode, specification files, vibe coding—can and should transfer to writing contexts. Engages directly with Ted Chiang's educational skepticism while demonstrating that thinking relocates rather than disappears when AI handles execution. Many student posts directly respond to or extend his arguments.
