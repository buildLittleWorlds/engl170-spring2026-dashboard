Overview

Dominic takes a philosophical, theory-driven approach to AI, drawing on cognitive science frameworks like Cognitive Load Theory and the Extended Mind thesis. He argues that not all struggle is equally valuable—the goal should be directing mental energy toward high-level thinking rather than mechanical tasks. Active engagement with classmates' arguments, particularly Gabriel Bell's work on human relevancy.

Key Themes

  • Cognitive load theory
  • Extended mind thesis
  • Efficiency vs. growth
  • Higher-level thinking
  • Mental health & attention
  • Constructive vs. empty struggle
