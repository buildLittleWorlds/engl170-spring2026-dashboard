Overview

Jinx brings a psychology lens to the AI conversation, examining how digital technologies affect mental health, cognition, and emotional well-being. Their blog "Psyched for Psychology" explores the intersection of technology and the human mind, with particular attention to how social media algorithms and AI fluency create psychological hazards. Strong emphasis on metacognitive awareness as defense.
