Overview

Sam explores AI through multiple lenses—from writing tools to ocean research to sports officiating. His approach balances skeptical engagement with Eliana Nodari's forklift metaphor while arguing that AI can serve as guidance rather than shortcut. Draws on Ethan Mollick's "Co-Intelligence" framework to distinguish between AI that replaces thinking and AI that supports developing learners.
