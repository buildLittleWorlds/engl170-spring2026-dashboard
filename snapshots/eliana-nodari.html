Overview

Eliana tackles the AI conversation with rigorous research and comparative analysis. She systematically dismantles viral claims about AI's environmental impact by contextualizing data against legacy industries, and argues that AI can be a tool for environmental remediation rather than degradation. Her writing balances skepticism about hyperbolic claims with genuine engagement with critics like Ted Chiang.

Posts

Myths and Truths about AI and the Environment January 15, 2026

Recontextualizes AI environmental claims against global industrial footprints. Shows that the textile industry, traditional banking, and e-waste crisis dwarf AI's impact. Argues AI is better understood as a tool for environmental optimization than a threat.

Using AI to Elevate Thinking January 14, 2026

Explores Ted Chiang's "forklift in the weight room" metaphor and argues for nuanced engagement. Contends that AI can elevate thinking when used to handle mechanical tasks, freeing mental energy for higher-order analysis.

Expanding on the Effects of Banning AI January 13, 2026

Examines the downstream effects of blanket AI bans in education. Argues that prohibition fails to prepare students for professional environments where AI tools are standard, creating a skills gap rather than protecting cognition.
