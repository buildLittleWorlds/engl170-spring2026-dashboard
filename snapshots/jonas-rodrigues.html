Overview

Jonas explores AI through extended metaphors and cross-domain applications, from ancient history to everyday navigation. His "GPS and the Driver" metaphor has become a touchstone in the network, refining the conversation about AI as tool vs. crutch. He emphasizes that using AI well requires knowing when it's wrong—expertise enables productive partnership.
