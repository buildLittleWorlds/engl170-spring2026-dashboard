Overview

Kevion brings the AI conversation into sports, specifically examining how AI is transforming basketball officiating. He raises fundamental questions about whether "perfect" officiating improves or diminishes the game, exploring tensions between accuracy and human judgment, objectivity and drama. His work connects course themes about AI and human intuition to a domain where the stakes feel visceral.
