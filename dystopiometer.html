The Scale

Each post is scored from 1 to 10 based on its orientation toward the future. Does the writer see progress as inevitable, or doom? Are humans empowered agents or passive subjects of forces beyond their control?

1–2
Bright Utopian
Progress is inevitable. Technology will save us. Human flourishing is the default.
3–4
Cautious Optimist
Things are improving, with caveats. Challenges are solvable. Agency is real.
5–6
Balanced
Holds hope and concern in tension. Sees trade-offs. Doesn't commit.
7–8
Concerned Pessimist
Dominant worry. Something valuable is eroding. Solutions may not be adopted.
9–10
Full Dystopian
Doom is inevitable. Forces beyond control. Resistance may be futile.
